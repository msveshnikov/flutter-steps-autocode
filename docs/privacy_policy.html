Steps Tracker Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Current Date]

1. Introduction

Steps Tracker is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Steps Tracker does not require you to sign in or provide any personal information. We only collect and store the following data locally on your device:

  • Step count
  • Calories burned
  • Walking distance
  • Active time

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing you with step tracking and fitness statistics within the app. We do not share or transmit this data to any external servers or third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device. If you choose to use the optional Google Drive backup feature, your data will be securely stored in your personal Google Drive account.

5. Third-Party Services

Steps Tracker offers optional integration with Samsung Health and Google Fit. If you choose to use these integrations, please refer to their respective privacy policies for information on how they handle your data.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
